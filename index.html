Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Control

1. Local Data Storage

Smart Menu is an offline-first application. All your order history and menu data are stored directly on your device using browser local storage. We do not transmit your data to any external servers.

2. Zero Tracking

We do not use cookies, trackers, or any third-party analytics. Your activities within the app are strictly private to you and your device.

3. Security

Admin functions are protected by a local passcode. It is your responsibility to keep this passcode secure to prevent unauthorized changes to your menu or history.
